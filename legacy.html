Our Legacy

IEDC unit of Thalassery Engineering college has been conducting hackatly for past few years. We have been successful in encouraging great ideas from great minds.

Legacy of IEDC

Hackatly 22 (2022): First hackathon launched by IEDC Thalassery.

Hackatly 23 (2023): Expanded participation and complex challenges.

Hackatly 3.0 (2025): Biggest edition yet with broader themes.

Hackatly 4.0 (Now): Continuing the legacy of innovation and excellence.

Our History

Since Hackatly 22 in 2022, IEDC Thalassery has successfully hosted annual hackathons, each year growing in scale, attracting more participants and industry support.


What started as a small inter-college event has now evolved into a national-level hackathon, bringing together young innovators from all colleges across India.

Hackatly 3.0 (2025)

Our most recent edition — a national-level hackathon with 10 diverse themes and record participation.
