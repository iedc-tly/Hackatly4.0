F.A.Qs

1. Where is it?

Hackatly will be taking place at College of Engineering Thalassery, Eranholi P.O., Thalassery-670107.

2. Who can attend?

Be a beginner or a pro, anyone of the age 18 or above can attend Hackatly. All are welcomed and encouraged to join us.

3. Do I need to know coding?

Coding experience is not at all necessary. Non-STEM majors, first-time hackers, and beginners are welcomed and encouraged to join us. We will have mentors to guide you.

4. When will the registration close?

The registration closes on March 20th. So hurry up!!!!!

5. What should I bring?

Bring your college ID card, laptop, and all other necessary items you need to use.

6. Do I need a team?

If you are planning to compete for the prize, you may be in a team of 2-4 people. The members of your team can also be from different colleges.

7. How do I register?

Click on the registration button, fill out the form (make sure that all the details are entered correctly) and we will get you ready to hack!
